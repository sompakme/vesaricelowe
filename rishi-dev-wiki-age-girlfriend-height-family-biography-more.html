Career

Dev started taking interest in acting when he was a five years old kid. He appears in many Punjabi TV series. He has also performed theatre for about 5 years before the TV series. He also sings and composes some songs which are uploaded on his YouTube channel. He sings a Hindi version of Justin Bieber’s song ‘Peaches’ and uploads it on Twitter.

As an Actor

Rishi started his career in 2012 with I Luv My India but he could not get much attention. He became popular in the country with the Balika Vadhu TV series in 2015, in which he played the role of Gopal. This series gave him name and fame. 

From 2017-2018, he worked in the famous TV series ‘Yeh Rishta kya Kehlata Hai’. He acted as Naksh Singhania along with Mohena Singh, who played the role of Keerti. Rishi as Naksh was the brother of lead actress Nayra in the series. According to the Times of India, he leaves the show in between but the reason is not revealed yet.

He has also appeared in many other shows such as Bani – Ishq Da Kalma, Yeh Hai Aashiqui, Bade Bhaiyya Ki Dulhania, and many more. His acting leads him to meet well-known actors of the industry.

YouTuber

Rishi Dev made his account on 07 December 2017 under the head Rimorav Vlogs. The channel has more than 5 million subscribers and 1.5 billion views. He uploads music videos, life hacks, creativity, pranks, and challenges videos on his YouTube channel. He made videos with his two siblings named Diksha Sharma and Sanjay Sharma.

He uploads a music video titled ‘Official Music Video | TONIGHT | Rimorav Vlogs’ which is watched by 8.4 million people and 263k people liked the video till May 2021. Most of the video has millions of views and thousands of likes. 

Earlier, he used to make videos with his friends named Mohena Singh and Gaurav Wadhwa. They named the channel Rimorav in which ‘Ri’ means Rishi, ‘mo’ means Mohena, and ‘rav’ means Gaurav. One day, a conflict occurs between the three and they left the channel.
