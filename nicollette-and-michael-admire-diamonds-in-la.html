Nicollette and Michael admire diamonds in LA

Publish date: 2024-07-28

    After being snapped frolicking on a Hawaii beach last week Nicollette Sheridan and her crooner beau, Michael Bolton, have been stepping out publicly in LA. The attractive pair, who are rekindling their Nineties romance, lent their sparkling company to Harry Winston when the jeweller-to-the-stars threw open the doors of its new store on Beverly Hills' Rodeo Drive.

    The fabulous 42-year-old - who keeps herself trim with an action-packed life that includes skiing, riding and swimming - had chosen a sexy, thigh-revealing leather skirt for the event.

    Her new/old love Michael made his name in the late Eighties with ballads like How Am I Supposed To Live Without You, but has since swapped the trademark locks that won him legions of fans for a "mature gent" look.

    After posing happily for photos, Nicollette got down to the business in hand, trying on a dazzling diamond necklace.

    Also admiring the rocks on display were Nicollette's fellow housewife, Eva Longoria and newly married burlesque performer Dita Von Teese, who tied the knot with singer Marilyn Manson at a December ceremony in Ireland.
