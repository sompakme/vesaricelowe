Ive Been Wearing These Barely There, Buttery Soft Thongs for 10+ Years

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Thongs. I must admit, that word doesn’t typically make me think of comfort. But there’s one thong in particular that’s had me giving heart eyes for over a decade, and it’s by Honeydew Intimates.

I first discovered the Honeydew Intimates Ahna Lace Thongs during a trip to Los Angeles, where I simply had to visit then-uber-popular store Kitson in Beverly Hills. (Any store that was frequented by celebs like Mischa Barton circa The OC and Lindsay Lohan circa Mean Girls was, of course, a must-see.) I noticed the Honeydew thongs the moment I stepped through the door. 

For starters, the hipster silhouette is low rise, so it doesn’t poke out of jeans or skirts — but not so much that it’s too far below my belly button, either. The thong itself isn’t too thin or string-like; it actually has some fabric to it so you don’t feel like you’re sporting a string of dental floss. The blend of rayon and spandex jersey is super soft, too — it feels like butter against the skin. Each one is finished with a contrasting scalloped lace trim and a pretty miniature bow detail at the front. They come in sizes S to XL — for reference, I consistently buy the XL, which skims and settles perfectly over my curvy waist and hips.

You can snag a three-pack of the thongs (which includes one gray and two black options) for $36 at Nordstrom. These thongs aren’t just a favorite of mine, either, since they’ve racked up multiple five-star ratings on the site. Shoppers say they’re “stretchy in all the right ways,” “wonderful quality,” and “amazingly comfortable.” One reviewer raved that they’re “incredibly soft and comfortable — perfect for everyday wear at work.” (Considering that — TMI — I’m wearing them as I type this, I definitely agree.)

Ever since I first walked out of Kitson with my Honeydew Intimates thong three-pack all those years ago, I’ve been hooked. Every time I slip one on, I just feel like my absolute best self. Intrigued? Pick up a pack for yourself at Nordstrom, and shop more options from the brand, below.
