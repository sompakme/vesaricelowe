    ‘Sunday scaries’ are non-existent when Jennifer Lopez, Oprah Winfrey and Kim Kardashian are providing us with epic dinner party selfies from Anastasia Soare’s party to celebrate her beauty brand Anastasia Beverly Hills’ 25th anniversary.

    JLo has provided us with images of the party guest collaboration we didn't know we needed. The term ‘squad goals’ has become an integral part of our vocabulary, and this trio have encapsulated the true meaning of the phrase. Their selfies are everything, and we have never wanted to be part of a friendship group more than this one.

    JLo shared mutiple selfies of the mega-trio

    Lopez uploaded a plethora or snaps from the evening (much to our delight) last night saying: "Lovely dinner last night celebrating Anastasia’s 25th anniversary ?." The post has unsurprisingly amassed over 1.3 million likes in less than 12 hours.

    Full hollywood-glam was on the dress-code agenda, as the actress wore a sheer mini dress with gold embellishments and a white feather jacket. She paired the elegant look with chunky gold platform heels and embellished dangle earrings.

    Beauty brand ‘Skkn’ founder Kim K opted for a chic glittery mahogany bandeau dress, paired with two diamante cross chains. Both she and JLo sported the ultimate opulent make-up looks with a nude lip and killer eyelashes combo. Don’t they look like sisters?

    The crème de la crème to this epic fashion-set sandwich was TV royalty Oprah Winfrey sat between the two. As if the trio had coordinated their sartorial colour palette, she dazzled in a dark brown sequin blazer layered over a white vest. All three wore their hair in variations of a sleek updo, looking like the most elegant trio. Move over Charlie’s Angels.

    @anastasiasoare

    Beauty entrepreneur Anastasia Soare posted behind the scenes videos of the event, where long tables adorned with pink peony and white rose floral arrangements, and pink candlesticks with glass tealights adorned the centre of the table. Caviar, truffle gnocchi, gold-leaved fish fillets and specially curated cocktails were on the stellar food and drinks agenda. 

    The guest list was as magnificent as the food menu, with Sofia Vergara, Sharon Stone and Jessica Alba amongst the fashion-set in attendance.
