Will there be a third season of XO, Kitty?

The good news is that yes, there will be a third season of XO, Kitty. Netflix officially confirmed the renewal of the show on December 14, 2023, in a video announcement featuring the main cast members. The video showed Anna Cathcart (who plays Kitty) holding a sign that read “XO, Kitty Season 3 is coming!” and the rest of the cast cheering and celebrating. You can watch the video here.

The bad news is that the third season will also be the final season of the show. According to the creator and executive producer Jenny Han, who is also the author of the To All the Boys books, the decision to end the show after three seasons was made to give the story a satisfying and meaningful conclusion. She said in a statement:

We are so grateful to Netflix for allowing us to tell Kitty’s story and share it with the world. We are also so thankful to our amazing cast and crew, who have poured their hearts and souls into this show. We have had so much fun creating this show and we hope that the fans will enjoy the final season as much as we did. We are sad to say goodbye to Kitty and her friends, but we are also excited to see them grow and find their happy endings.
