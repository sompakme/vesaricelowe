Rhea Ripley Calls Dominik Mysterio Her Dinner

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Rhea Ripley is one of the most beloved female WWE Superstars right now as she has established herself as someone worth following. Her relationship with Dominik Mysterio continues to be a highlight for many and now it appears she called Mysterio her dinner in a new video.

The Judgment Day is comprised of Finn Balor, Damian Priest, Rhea Ripley and Dominik Mysterio. Together, they have been wreaking havoc in WWE. Rhea Ripley and Mysterio have seen the most growth during their time in the faction.

Their on-screen romance has gotten over to the point that many fans ship them in real life as well. The two never fail to show their love and affection towards each other no matter what.

As seen in a new video posted on TikTok, Rhea Ripley was chewing Dominik Mysterio’s hair and was referring to him as her dinner. Naturally, fans absolutely loved this video.

Rhea Ripley also wants to get tattoo of Dominik Mysterio on her heart. Dominik Mysterio also called her his favorite snack recently. We’ll have to wait and see how their relationship will continue to develop in the coming weeks.
