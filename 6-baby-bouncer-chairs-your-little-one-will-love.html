6 baby bouncer chairs your little one will love

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Soothe and entertain your baby with these must-have chairs

    A bouncer chair is a must-have bit of kit for parents - it both stimulates and entertains your baby while giving you some valuable time hands-free to do household chores, cook, or even just drink a cup of tea while it's still hot.

    SHOP: Best prams, pushchairs and double prams for your baby – including brands loved by Kate and Meghan

    But with so many options available, what should you choose?

    Some feature colourful toys, various motion settings and soothing sounds, whereas others are more simple (and stylish) in their design. The model you buy ultimately depends on your personal taste, as well as how you intend to use a bouncer chair for your baby.

    BabyBjorn Bliss Bouncer

    A brand loved by parents including Joe Wicks, the BabyBjorn Bliss is suitable for babies from birth up to two years, and features an ergonomically-shaped seat to provide support to your baby's back, neck and head. The lightweight seat has three different positions for play, rest and sleep, and folds flat so you can also take it with you when you go away.

    BabyBjorn Bliss Bouncer, £189, John Lewis

    Nuna LEAF Grow

    With three recline positions, the LEAF Grow is an investment that both you and your little one will love for years to come. A small push will give your baby the feeling of being rocked in your arms for over two minutes, with a gentle side-to-side motion, while the seat can also be locked for feeding or playtime.

    Nuna LEAF Grow, £260, John Lewis

    Wave Rocker Baby Bouncer Chair

    Nap times will be even better with this Wave rocking cradle, which provides soothing motions and a three-position reclining seat, along with four melodies to create a relaxing atmosphere for your baby. A removable toy bar offers post-naptime entertainment, and better still, the seat folds flat and is easy to store away when not in use.

    Wave Rocker Baby Bouncer Chair, £79.20, Mamas & Papas

    Graco All Ways Soother Swing Horizon

    There is much to love about this chair, which not only has ten built-in melodies and five nature sounds, but also 16 calming motions, six swinging gears and two speeds of gentle vibrations. There is also a detachable toy bar with two toys, a multi-recline seat to keep your baby comfortable during rest and play, and a support strap to ensure they are secure at all times. Well worth the investment.

    Graco All Ways Soother Swing Horizon, £224.99, Amazon

    Mamas & Papas Capella Bouncing Cradle

    How cute is this bouncing cradle?! Not only does it have an adorable cloud design, but it also features a gentle bouncing function activated by your baby's movement, along with four different melodies to relax your little one and soothe them to sleep.

    And that's not all; there is also a removable toy bar with two interactive toys, along with a safety harness and head-hugging pillow. 

    Capella bouncing cradle, £55.20, Mamas & Papas

    SHOP NOW

    Joie Dreamer Rocker

    This baby rocker is great for newborn and up. It has three positions for the reclining seat which rocks or bounces with a simple push or when the baby moves.

    It's fantastic for travel as its lightweight design folds up, and it also plays five classical lullabies and five nature sounds paired with vibration and toys to sooth little ones. Holds up to 15kgs.

    Joie Dreamer Rocker, £75, Boots
