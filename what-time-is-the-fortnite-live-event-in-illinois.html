Okay, Okay, But How Do I Know the Time in Illinois When the Event Actually Happens?

Now we're talkin'! Since Fortnite live events are usually a global affair, they typically happen at the same time for everyone. But fear not, because we've got the handy-dandy time conversion magic for you Illinois folks:

  • Epic announces the event time in Eastern Time (ET): That's the time zone for New York City and the gang.
  • Illinois is in Central Time (CT): Which means you gotta subtract one hour from the announced ET time to get your Illinois Fortnite party time.

For example: If Epic announces a live event for 8 PM ET, then giddy up, Illinois Flossers, because the party starts at 7 PM CT!
