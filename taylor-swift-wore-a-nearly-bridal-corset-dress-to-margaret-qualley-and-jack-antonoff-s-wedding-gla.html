Taylor Swift Wore a Nearly Bridal Corset Dress to Margaret Qualley and Jack Antonoff's Wedding| Gla

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Taylor Swift did not break wedding guest etiquette at Jack Antonoff and Margaret Qualley’s intimate New Jersey wedding…but she came a little close.

On August 19, the 33-year-old pop star arrived at the Long Beach Island nuptials wearing an asymmetrical Erdem lace midi-dress that verges on white in some sunny outdoor photos, but is clearly light blue to the naked eye. The dress—which originally retailed for $4,195 at Saks before going on sale for $1,258.50—featured a corset-inspired bustier top with exposed boning, a ribbon belt, and a flouncy, sheer lace skirt. Swift looked stunning in her wedding guest ensemble, which she paired with sparkling blue heels and her signature red lips. You can see photos here.

Likely due to an influx of gawkers that have been swarming Long Beach Island to catch a glimpse of Swift, the singer reportedly entered Antonoff’s wedding venue through the back doors. In the days leading up to the nuptials, which included a rehearsal dinner on August 18, many social media users have criticized the crowds of fans for invading the pop star’s privacy and disrupting her friend’s personal event. Many have called on these so-called fans to rewatch Swift’s vulnerable documentary, Miss Americana, or listen to her songs “I Know Places” and “The Lakes” before repeating this behavior.

Of course, Swift was not the only famous face at the wedding. The 28-year-old bride’s mother and Maid costar Andie MacDowell attended the celebration alongside other celebrities, including Zoë Kravitz, Channing Tatum, and Lana Del Rey. For her part, Qualley wore a white silk halter dress and Mary Jane flats while the 39-year-old groom wore a black suit.
