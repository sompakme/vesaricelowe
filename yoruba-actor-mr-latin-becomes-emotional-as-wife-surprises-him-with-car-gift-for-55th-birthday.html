Yoruba Actor Mr Latin Becomes Emotional As Wife Surprises Him With Car Gift for 55th Birthday

Publish date: 2024-07-25
  • Comedian Mr Latin has taken to social media in appreciation of his wife who recently surprised him in a pleasant way
  • The Nollywood veteran was gifted a brand new Audi SUV several weeks after he clocked the age of 55
