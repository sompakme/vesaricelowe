Kendrick Lamar net worth: What is the fortune of the Grammy-winning rapper?

The name Kendrick Lamar is now synonym of other names such as Dr. Dre, Tupac, Sneep Dogg, Notorious B.I.G. or Eminem. Kendrick was already considered a great rapper but this past year made him one of the greats.

With all that critical success, there is also the financial success that comes with selling millions of albums or makes him one of the most listened artists on every streaming platform.
