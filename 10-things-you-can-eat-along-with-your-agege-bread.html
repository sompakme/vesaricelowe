10 things you can eat along with your Agege bread

Publish date: 2024-09-24

We know some of you are ardent lovers of Agege bread; it is even useless trying to act posh again when it comes to eating that bread because everyone knows you can kill for it even though you can afford to buy the regular sliced bread.

We really cannot blame you because Agege bread would always bask in its glory. Seeing it being hawked in the street or perceiving its sweet smell after baking would make you want to eat it no matter what. It has been saving people from hunger since forever as one could afford to buy one.

As a matter of fact, we are sure many Lagosians must have died of hunger if not for the role of Agege bread in the society. And there is a way the sellers arrange the loaves on their trays; the butter container would always been seen in the middle with the loaves being arranged round it.

Agege bread when just baked is super fluffy; even after a few days, you would still enjoy the delicious bread as you could work the fluffiness back with your hands unlike the sliced bread that would start to break after the first day. It is sensational getting to buy this bread and cutting it; the pulling strength is seen then.

Lastly, Agege bread is delicious; find below some of the things you can eat your Agege bread with:

1. Akara

So you are a one way person and believe Akara can only be eaten when taking pap, dey there o. If you try eating your Agege bread with hot Akara, we doubt if you would want to try eating any other food again.

This combination is divine and would always turn you to Oliver Twist. The bread gets a new definition after this combination as we are sure your life would never remain the same.

2. Tea

Eating your Agege bread while drinking tea is good enough; but please we hope you are not part of the people that drown the bread in hot tea. Dipping the bread into the tea is simply evil as you would never get the taste of the delicious bread that way. If the tea is iced tea like the type they sell in Ilorin markets, then you would enjoy it.

3. Beans

Agege bread can be eaten with beans; not just any type of beans though, Ewa agonyin is the perfect beans to eat your bread with. Ahh, no word can adequately describe the taste and we are sure you would feel sleepy right after. It has a way of relaxing the nerves.

When Agege bread is combined with Ewa agonyin, the meal is highly satisfactory. The beans has its unique taste and same goes for the Agege bread, combining the two would give you a unique taste you can never get with each independently.

4. Fried eggs

This is what most people are familiar with. They prefer eating their Agege bread with fried or scrambled eggs. Depending on how creative you are, the meal could become extraordinary especially if you can fry the eggs and make everyone in the neighborhood locate your house with the smell the way an aboki would disturb your olfactory lobes, then you are in for a treat.

5. Coca-Cola

Please pay no attention to the saying that bread and coke is the perfect food for the manual laborers. Bricklayers are human beings too; stop this discrimination and enjoy your meal. Eating your Agege bread with a bottle of coke would give you another feeling, you can be sure you would taste the feeling when an Agege bread is in view.

6. Avocado

If you have not tried eating your Agege bread with avocado or pear, you are seriously missing. Try it as soon as you can and your life would never remain the same.

7. Stew

You can also eat your Agege bread with stew; it could be plain stew or fish stew. The food value you tend to get from this is awesome. We do not need to tell you the classes of food and how it affects your health, do we?

8. Groundnut

Eating your Agege bread with groundnut is meal on the go. Some people snack on it by making it an in between meal. It is delightful chewing the nuts at the same time you eat the bread. It is a very unique combination.

9. Sardine

If you are a student, then you would probably know this combination. Your meal is rather in two minutes and you would enjoy the meal if your Agege bread is piping hot or straight from the oven. Sardine and Agege bread have been saving lives since 1805.

10. Water

Remember water is a universal solvent; taken your Agege bread with cold water will still do the magic and save you from dying of hunger. You would be satisfied nevertheless, just tune in your mind and consider all the minerals available in water when you are eating your Agege bread.
