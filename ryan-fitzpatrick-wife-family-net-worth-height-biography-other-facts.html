Career

After his outstanding records, the St. Louis Rams were the first to come for Ryan, who brought him to the seventh round of the 2005 NFL Draft. He was with the St. Louis Rams until 2007, when he was traded to the Cincinnati Bengals for a seventh-round pick in the 2008 NFL Draft.

Despite his many struggles on the team, Ryan is remembered in Western New York with Bills fans truly appreciating him for all of his accomplishments throughout his Buffalo career.

In 2013, he signed a two-year contract with the Tennessee Titans, replacing Jake Locker for four weeks following an injury he suffered in the third quarter. Fitzpatrick completed 3 of Locker’s 8 passes for 108 yards, including a 77-yard pass to Nate Washington, in a 38-13 win over the Jets.
