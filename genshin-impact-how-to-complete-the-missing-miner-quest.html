Genshin Impact - How to complete the missing miner quest

Publish date: 2024-08-31

In The Missing Miner quest, Travelers will have to help Jinwu find Uncle He, who went missing long ago. Here's his exact location and guide to complete the quest.

Page Updated April 26, 2022, 12:31 | First Published March 31, 2022, 12:31

The missing miner is a Genshin Impact quest: while exploring the underground mines of The Chasm, Jinwu will tell you about Uncle He, a miner who went missing a long time ago. While others think he is dead, Jinwu believes the opposite and asks you to find him using the Dairy Fragments as clues. 

Read on for the Missing Miner quest's location and detailed walkthough.

Dairy fragments -Location and How to Collect

The locations of Dairy Fragments will get marked on the map, and they are easy to collect. After that, you will have to talk to Jinwu, and once you do it, you can then follow this guide to get to Uncle He's location and trigger The Missing Miner quest.

The Missing Miner quest -

Genshin Impact: Uncle He's location

1. The first thing you need to do is teleport to the location marked on the map above using the teleport waypoint that will land you in a cave.

2. You will then have to start moving towards the right side of the cave and then again take a right when you spot two camps.

3. You will then reach Underground Waterway, where you need to jump down on a tree's branch marked in the image. 

4. Start walking down that path, and you will find a Work Handbook lying on a table. Once you read it, you will find the miner's name on it, and once you complete reading it, you will hear whispering coming from behind you.

5. A man will start talking to you, and during your conversation, you will get to know that he is Uncle He. After you complete the dialogue with him, you will have to find mushroom meat for Uncle He by heading to the location marked on the map. 

6. After that, you will have to defeat the floating fungi, and once you do it, a dialogue will get started with Uncle He. After you complete the dialogue, take him back to Jinwu at her camp.

7. Talk to Junyi one last time to complete The Missing Miner quest.
