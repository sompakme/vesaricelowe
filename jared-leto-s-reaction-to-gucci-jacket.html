Jared Leto's Reaction To Gucci Jacket

Publish date: 2024-07-05

It is perhaps the greatest love story of our time — that of Jared Leto and his green Gucci jacket, which the actor wore to the European premiere of Suicide Squad in London on Wednesday afternoon.

Leto’s choice to wear the label was no surprise, given his best-friend status with creative director Alessandro Michele. The Oscar winner has been wearing Gucci almost exclusively during the film’s press tour, but the kelly green coat and pastel pink trousers clearly have a special place in his heart.

The love-at-first-sight moment was captured by a front-row attendee at the brand’s men’s show in Milan earlier this year, and the rest, as they say, is history.
