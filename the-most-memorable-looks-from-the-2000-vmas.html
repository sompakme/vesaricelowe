The most memorable looks from the 2000 VMAs

Publish date: 2024-10-02
By Heather Cichowski

The millennium might not seem like it was that long ago, but we have seen 20 different MTV Video Music Awards ceremonies since then.

The 2000 VMAs featured a number of celebrities who are still popular today, including Jennifer Lopez, Heidi Klum and Pink, as well as some beloved girl and boy groups.

A lot of the faces might be recognizable, but clothing trends have changed in two decades. And given that this is the MTV VMAs, stars went out to wow, shock and amaze with their looks.
