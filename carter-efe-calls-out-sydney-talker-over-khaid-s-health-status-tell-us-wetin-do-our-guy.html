Carter Efe Calls Out Sydney Talker Over Khaids Health Status: Tell Us Wetin Do Our Guy

Publish date: 2024-07-18
  • Popular Nigerian skit maker Carter Efe left many worried as called out his friend and colleague Sydney Talker over the well-being of singer Khaid
  • Recall that the fast-rising artist trended on social media after a video of him on a hospital bed went viral 
  • During a recent live video, Carter noted that Sydney, who manages the young talent, has refused to pick up the calls of anybody since the sudden update 

Popular Nigerian skit maker Carter Efe has raised the alarm concerning the state of health of fast-rising singer Sulaimon Shekoni Solomon aka Khaid.

Legit.ng reported a few days ago that a video of the young artist in pain while being rushed to a hospital went viral on social media.

The news spread that he was diagnosed with internal bleeding, although his management has yet to release an official statement regarding his current health condition.

Carter Efe openly confronted his colleague, Sydney Talker, about the health of his record label signee, Khaid.

Sydney previously made a post begging Nigerians to pray for Khaid, without divulging more details 

Carter, a close friend of Sydney, turned to Instagram Live to criticise him for attempting to keep quiet about the issue.

He claimed he has been contacting Sydney for follow-up and hasn’t gotten any response, the same as many others who have reached out to him to inquire about Khaid.
