Amy Schumer Shares Photo of Her Boyfriend After the Golden Globes

Publish date: 2024-10-02

Amy Schumer may not have gone home with a Golden Globe for her performance in Trainwreck, but that doesn't mean she didn't have an incredible night. The comedienne took to Instagram to document her awards show experience, sharing photos of the people that made her night extra special. While she posted plenty of double-tap worthy shots of her family and BFF Jennifer Lawrence, our favorite snap has to be the one she 'grammed this afternoon of her and her boyfriend Ben Hanisch snuggling after the after-parties.

In the photo, the too-cute couple are sitting hand-in-hand in a dimly-lit restaurant, as he whispers something in her ear. She captioned the pic by thanking everyone who made the night possible—including Chardonnay.

"Thanks @prabalgurung for my dress and @andrea_tiller for my face and @kimmykuppkakes for my hairdo and @deborahlippmann For my nails and @leesaevansstyle for my styling and @carriebyalick for getting me in that door and Chardonnay for tasting so good and @kimcaramele for being my date and @benhanisch for being my after date and Melvin and Kokomo for driving us and pasta," she wrote (below).
