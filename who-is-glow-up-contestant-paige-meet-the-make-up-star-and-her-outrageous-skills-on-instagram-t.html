Who is Glow Up’s Paige?

Paige is a contestant on the BBC’s make-up competition series Glow Up.

The 20-year-old is from London and outside of the show, she works as a retail assistant.

Paige’s mum wanted her to become a lawyer or a doctor and follow in the footsteps of her brother and sister. However, Paige decided to follow her dream and pursue a career in make-up.

Is Paige on Instagram?

Yes, Paige is on Instagram. Her username is @paintedbypaigee.

On her Instagram account, you’ll find pictures and videos from her time on Glow Up, her everyday life, as well as snaps of her incredible make-up creations.

Paige also has her very own Youtube channel, where you can enjoy her make-up tutorial and transformation videos.
