Do cheese sticks need to be refrigerated?

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Answer

When not being cooked with or eaten, cheese sticks should always be stored in the refrigerator. Cheese sticks may only be kept out at room temperature if they are well sealed from the elements, are not exposed to strong heat, and are not left out for more than two hours.
