WWE veteran Natalya and sister Jenni vow not to wear underwear during 2021 in latest YouTube video

Publish date: 2024-07-05

WWE veteran Natalya Neidhart and her sister Jenni have vowed not to wear underwear in 2021.

The Neidhart sisters had a lot to celebrate in their recent YouTube video released on Monday: the start of a new year, and marking one year of their YouTube channel.

In the video, the two discuss their desires for the new year: Natalya wanting to be come WWE SmackDown women's champion and Jenni reminding fans that she is "single and ready to mingle" and wants to find a boyfriend this year.

The sisters said they want to gain five pounds – rather than lose – to be "thicker than a snicker" because "thick is in."

The two daughters of WWE Hall of Famer Jim "The Anvil" Neidhart end their video, entitled New Year Vibes!!!, with Jenni doing a split in a gown.

After Natalya notices Jenni isn't wearing underwear and Jenni points out Natalya isn't wearing any either, both making the final resolution: "We are not wearing underwear in all 2021, you got it?"

Natalya and Jenni's YouTube subscribers are used to the sisters' antics, usually in beautiful low-cut dresses, performing wrestling moves on each other, all-you-can-eat contests, and intimate Q&As while sitting in bed together.

Over the last year, their channel has gained over 56,000 subscribers.

Jenni is not a wrestler like her older sister Natalya, father, and uncle, Bret Hart, instead she is a self-proclaimed "culinary and ambience enthusiast."

Natalya is currently competing on WWE Smackdown on Fridays 8.00pm ET on Fox.
