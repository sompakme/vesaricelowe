Cody Rhodes Surprises Army Veteran with Special Table Gift After 11/24 WWE SmackDown

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Cody Rhodes is certainly one of the most beloved stars in WWE right now. Cody’s natural charisma and relatable attitude have made him a fan-favorite in WWE. He gained more favor from fans after Smackdown concluded this week, as he surprised a fan with a table gift.

Ringside News provided complete coverage of WWE SmackDown this week. The episode built towards Survivor Series in a big way, as fans are already excited for the Premium Live Event.

Cody Rhodes was a vocal point of the show and he didn’t stop being a loveable babyface after Smackdown concluded. Cody Rhodes and Seth Rollins faced Dominik Mysterio and JD McDonagh in a tag team match.

After that was done, Cody Rhodes cut a promo on the crowd and he and Rollins even brought a table to the ring and wrote their autographs on it. Seth Rollins would then look through the entire Allstate Arena and spotted an armed force personnel sitting front row. Rollins and Rhodes decided to make the veteran’s day by gifting him the table.

This isn’t even the first time Cody Rhodes gifted table this year. Seth Rollins, Cody Rhodes, Sami Zayn, Jey Uso and Randy Orton will be squaring off against The Judgment Day at Survivor Series in the WarGames match. It remains to be seen whose team will prevail in the match.
