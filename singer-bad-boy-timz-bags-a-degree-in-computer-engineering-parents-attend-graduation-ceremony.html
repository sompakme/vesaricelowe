It is indeed a proud moment for the family members of Nigerian singer, Bad Boy Timz, who recently joined him to celebrate a massive achievement.

The young man who is actively involved in the music industry recently graduated from the Bells University of Technology, Ogun state.

Bad Boy Timz bagged a degree in computer engineering from the institution. The MJ crooner happily took to his page on Instagram to share the good news with fans.
