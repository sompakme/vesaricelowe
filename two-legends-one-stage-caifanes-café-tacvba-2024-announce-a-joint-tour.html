Two Legends, One Stage: Caifanes + Caf Tacvba 2024 Announce A Joint Tour

Publish date: 2024-09-12

Experience a concert that bridges nostalgia with contemporary cultural and musical significance.  

Tickets Available Starting Tuesday December 12 @ 10am (local time)

General Onsale Begins on Friday December 15 at 10am (Local Time) on LiveNation.com

In an unprecedented collaboration, two legendary Mexican bands, Caifanes and Cafe Tacvba, are set to embark on their first-ever joint tour in the United States. This historic musical journey promises fans an unforgettable experience as the two groups come together to celebrate their legacies and showcase the power of Mexican rock on an international stage. Produced by Live Nation, the 20-city tour kicks off on May 29 and will go across the US until June 30.

Both bands originated in the late 80s in Mexico City. Despite their distinct musical styles, this tour is a testament to the enduring power of music, showcasing how these bands have evolved and continue to resonate with audiences across time.

ABOUT CAIFANES:

Caifanes have lived their path intensely and passionately since 1986. Evolution and changes have always been, and they will continue to be, part of their history. However, its constants remain: music and the endearing magic that several generations share and have tattooed on their hearts. That connection remains as powerful today as it was in the beginning. For young people, their music is new; for the older generation, those songs are already part of the soundtrack of their lives. The passion and dedication with which they listen and join their voices with the band remain intact and stronger than ever. Caifanes has always been the band that opens the doors to every band coming up behind them. Last two years and 108 concerts have seen the band getting better and bigger than ever, on stage no one rivals their power and strength. Their commitment to social causes has been an essential part of who they are from the start of the band.

ABOUT CAFE TACVBA:

Together since 1989 with the same founding members (Quique, Meme, Joselo and Ruben) Grammy winners and Mexico City indie rockers Café Tacvba are frightfully credited as the preeminent band to emerge from the “Rock en Español” movement of the early ‘90s. The Mexican quartet has remained one of the biggest acts on the Latin music scene since, inspiring generations of rockers in their wake. While critics look at these reasons to signify Café Tacvba’s importance, legions of global music followers are enamored with them simply because of their music, which is broadly appealing not only because of its groundbreaking nature, but also because of its fun, madcap and ever-changing.
