Voramas Srivaddhanaprabha Wiki, Biography, Net Worth, Age, Career, Relationship, Family

  • Voramas Srivaddhanaprabha, also known as Rux, is a successful Thai entrepreneur and fashion stylist based in Bangkok.
  • She is the first child of billionaire Vichai and Aimon Srivaddhanaprabha, overseeing the family business.

Tragedy Strikes

  • Voramas faced a devastating loss when her father, Vichai Srivaddhanaprabha, passed away in a helicopter crash in 2018.
  • The accident occurred outside the King Power Stadium, and though she was traveling with him, she managed to escape before the fatal crash.

Family Background and Education

  • Voramas was born on September 2, 1981, in Bangkok, Thailand, and is 37 years old as of 2018.
  • She has three siblings, a sister named Arunroong Srivaddhanaprabha and two brothers, Apichet Srivaddhanaprabha and Aiyawatt Srivaddhanaprabha.
  • Voramas pursued her education in England and later studied textile and fashion design at the University of London.

Marriage and Relationship

  • Their wedding ceremony was a grand affair attended by over a thousand guests.

Career and Net Worth

  • As a marketing head in Kings Group, she has played a crucial role in its success.

Physical Characteristics

A Tragic Loss

  • Vichai Srivaddhanaprabha was one of the top five richest people in Thailand, with a net worth of $4.5 billion.
