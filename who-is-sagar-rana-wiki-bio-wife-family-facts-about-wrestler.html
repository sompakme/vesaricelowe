Who is Sagar Rana? Wiki, Bio, Wife, Family &amp; Facts About Wrestler

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Sagar Rana (born October 14, 1998 – died May 4, 2021) was a popular Indian wrestler, media personality, bodybuilder, and national athlete from Haryana. He became popular in the country after winning several gold medals for the nation. 

On May 04, 2021, Sagar Rana was brutally killed by his senior wrestler named Sushil Kumar outside the Satpal Akhada Chattersal stadium. Moreover, an FIR has been lodged against Sushil, but he is absconding ever since a complaint has been filed against him. 

Biography

Sagar was born on October 14, 1998, in Munda Khera village of Jhajjar, Haryana, India. His other name is Sagar Dhankhar. 

He has completed his schooling at a local Government school in Jhajjar. Rana further took admission to the esteemed college of Haryana. He has a keen interest in bodybuilding and wrestling since childhood. 

Trivia / Wiki

Family & Caste

Rana belongs to a well-educated Jatt family of Haryana. According to the sources, his father is a head-constable and his mother is a housewife. However, Sagar has not shared the names of his parents in any public domain. 

He has an elder brother, who is the owner of a milk dairy in Jhajjar. He was a native of India. Rana belongs to the Rajput caste. 

Sagar Rana’s Relationship & Wife

Rana was a young and national-level athlete. Many of the people are curious to know about his wife. However, he has not shared anywhere that he was married. It could be possible that he was single. He was completely concentrated on his career. 

Death of Sagar Rana

Sagar Rana was ruthlessly beaten by Sushil Kumar and his friends, which leads the death of Rana. On May 04, 2021, he breathed his last in the Satpal Akhada Chattersal stadium of Delhi. An FIR was registered on the same day of his death in the Delhi Police Station but the accused, Sushil Kumar, got absconding. 

Sushil Kumar is a well-known wrestler, who has won the Olympic medal two times. It is also been said that Sushil and Sagar were verbally fighting on some topic which later converted into a bad fight. Delhi Police filed the case under many sections of the Indian Penal Code like 323 (voluntarily causing hurt), 506 (criminal intimidation), 120-B (criminal conspiracy), 341 (wrongful restraint), and many more. 

Sushil Kumar also demands pre-arrest bailed, but the request was dismissed by the judge. Police have also placed a reward of one lakh, who will share the location of Sushil Kumar. As per the reports of ANI, Sushil was spotted in the Meerut on May 06, 2021. 

Career

Sagar started practicing wrestling from the age of 6 years old. He maintained his body like a bodybuilder and decided to be a wrestler. As of 2020, he had played many competitions and won medals for the state and country. 

He won a gold medal in the ‘Junior National Championship’ in 2017 held in Patna. In the very next year, 2018, he again won the gold medal in the ‘Junior National Championship’ held in Jaipur. He competed in the ‘Senior National Championship’ in the year 2018 and won the gold medal for the country. 

Net Worth

Moreover, as he was a national champion, so he would get some allowances and privileges too. A rough estimate of Sagar’s net worth was at INR 3-4 Crores approximately.
