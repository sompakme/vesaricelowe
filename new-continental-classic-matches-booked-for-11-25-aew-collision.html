New Continental Classic Matches Booked For 11/25 AEW Collision

Publish date: 2024-08-01

AEW kicked off the Continental Classic this week during Dynamite The Gold League had a chance to show off this new idea to AEW fans. On Collision, the Blue League will get a chance.

Ringside News provided comprehsnsive coverage of AEW Dynamite this week. It was quite an episode as the company prepared fans for Thanksgiving. During the end of the show, they booked even more Continental Classic for Collision.

This week’s Collision will see the Continental Classic continue. The Blue League will have two matches. Daniel Garcia and Claudio Castagnoli will throw down in one match. That is not all.

In the other Continental Classic match, Eddie Kingston and Brody King will battle. That match could be a banger, and give fans a lot to see on a Saturday night.

AEW Collision will run head-to-head against WWE Survivor Series. That will be some tough competition, so we will have to see how AEW Collision turns out when everything is said and done.

11/25 AEW Collision Card

Eddie Kingston vs Brody King – Blue League in Continental Classic

Daniel Garcia vs Claudio Castagnoli – Blue League in Continental Classic
